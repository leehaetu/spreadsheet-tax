The problem

Many firm clients still live in spreadsheets. Moving every client into a full accounting suite is slow and expensive. Firms need a bridging product with clear review and authority — without pretending checkout or capacity are finished.

Vs full-suite rollout for every client: bridge spreadsheet-native clients first, with honest limits on online checkout and capacity claims.

  2. Try the same spreadsheet review path clients will see.

Security & HMRC status

Short answers. Full technical matrix lives under integrity for reviewers.

HMRC recognition

Not HMRC-recognised software. Not affiliated with HMRC. Sandbox and live APIs only when credentials and flags are set.

Your spreadsheet

Ongoing books stay in your file. When you upload, the file is processed to map period figures. That is different from a “device-only” claim — upload is required for mapping.

Accounts & access

  • Password accounts with HttpOnly session cookies
  • Optional authenticator MFA (TOTP) on the account page
  • Product pages require sign-in — no usable shell when signed out
  • Practice clients are scoped by firm membership

What is still open

  • Independent pen-test and full security launch-gate sign-off
  • Production malware scanning of uploads
  • Capacity proof for large practices
  • Card payments and guaranteed transactional email

See integrity map for implementer detail.

Quarterly vs year-end

Quarterly SE / UK / foreign property is the primary path. Year-end and BSAS tools exist as guided product stages; treat live HMRC outcomes as evidence-gated, not marketing claims.

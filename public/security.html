Security & HMRC status

Straight answers about your data and how we work with HMRC.

HMRC recognition

Spreadsheet Tax is independent software and is not currently on HMRC’s recognised software list. We are not affiliated with HMRC.

Your spreadsheet

You keep your day-to-day books in your own file. When you upload a period summary, we use it to prepare the figures you review — so the file is processed by the service, not left only on your device.

Your account

  • Sign-in with email and password
  • Optional authenticator app for extra protection
  • Your account pages only work when you are signed in
  • Practice clients stay within the firm you belong to

What you send to HMRC

Nothing goes to HMRC until you connect your HMRC login and choose to send. You approve the figures first. Preview checks stay inside Spreadsheet Tax.

The main path is quarterly updates. Year-end return steps are available in the app when you need them.

Uploading files

Your period spreadsheet is uploaded so we can map income and expenses for review. That mapping step is required before you approve and send.

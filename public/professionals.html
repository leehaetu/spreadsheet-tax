The problem

Bookkeepers inherit clients who will not move into a full ledger product. Every quarter becomes a chase for spreadsheets, manual mapping and unclear approval. You need one controlled review language for self-employed and property clients.

Vs forcing a full ledger migration: meet clients where they already work — spreadsheets — with the same plain-English approve-before-send path they can understand.

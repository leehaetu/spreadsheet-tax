How it works

Three steps from your period spreadsheet to a reviewed quarterly update package.

1

Upload your CSV or Excel period file (or try a sample).

2

Review income and expenses in plain English. Fix any blocking checks.

3

Confirm identifiers and send. Today’s public demo uses a safe preview path; live HMRC needs a later pilot gate.

What you will see in the app

After import, totals and source cards appear so you can check figures before sending.

  • Total income, expenses and net for the period
  • Self-employment, UK property and foreign property sections
  • Line-by-line mapping from your spreadsheet fields
  • Spreadsheet checks that block incomplete periods

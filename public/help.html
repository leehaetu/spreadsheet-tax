Help centre

How do I start without an account?

Open the app, download the free template or try a sample, review figures, and use the safe preview submit. Sign in when you want saved history.

What is the demo login?

demo@spreadsheet-tax.example / DemoPass123! — includes a sample practice workspace.

Is this live HMRC?

Public demo uses a safe preview path. Live HMRC needs pilot controls and OAuth credentials.

Where do my files go?

Period files are uploaded for mapping and review. Keep ongoing books in your own spreadsheet. See Security.

How do client portal links work?

In the workspace, open a client and choose Portal link. Share that URL with the client so they can see status and upload a period file.

I forgot my password

Use Forgot password. In demo, the reset link is written to server logs.

How do I invite a team member?

Sign in, open Admin, enter their email and role, then send the invite. They must register with the same email and open the accept link.

Where is the template?

Templates or direct CSV download.

Welcome to Spreadsheet Tax

This software bridges your spreadsheet to HMRC Making Tax Digital updates for you as an individual (self-employment, and UK or foreign property if HMRC already holds them). It mirrors income sources from HMRC — it does not create businesses or replace your books.

What Spreadsheet Tax does
  • Loads businesses from your HMRC connection
  • Uploads your spreadsheet for a quarterly period
  • Lets you check figures before you send
  • Shows HMRC Assist feedback after a tax calculation (year end)
What only HMRC does
  • Register self-employment or a UK/foreign property business
  • Change or cease an income source
  • Hold the legal record of your businesses

Want to report UK or foreign property as well as self-employment? Add or update those businesses in your HMRC online account, then load them here. Spreadsheet Tax will not invent a landlord business for you.

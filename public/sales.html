Frequently asked questions

What does “bridging-only” mean?

You keep your records in your own spreadsheet. Spreadsheet Tax maps those period figures into HMRC Making Tax Digital quarterly updates. It is not a full double-entry ledger product.

Which income sources are supported?

Self-employment (trade / business), UK property, and foreign property. You can use one source or combine them in a single period file.

Do my records leave my spreadsheet?

You upload a local file for mapping and submission. Your ongoing books stay in the spreadsheet you already maintain. Prefer the free template for the cleanest mapping.

Can my bookkeeper or accountant use it for clients?

Yes. There is a practitioner workspace, a multi-accountant practice dashboard, and a client portal for end clients of the firm.

Can my company sell this to our customers?

Yes under a company license. Licensed companies may sell subscriptions to their customers. All intellectual property belongs to Lee Hine, including modifications and improvements. See the license page for terms.

Is this affiliated with HMRC?

No. Spreadsheet Tax is independent software. Use of HMRC services is subject to HMRC terms and your own credentials and compliance obligations.

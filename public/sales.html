Common questions

  • Who is this for?

    Self-employed people, landlords, bookkeepers and firms who already work in spreadsheets — not a full accounting suite replacement.

  • Is it free?

    Yes to start. Experimental paid packages are listed under Pricing and are not for sale online yet.

  • Is it HMRC-recognised software?

    No. Independent software. You approve every figure before anything is sent. See Security.

  • Do you store my whole bookkeeping history?

    You keep day-to-day books in your spreadsheet. Period files are uploaded so we can map and review the quarter you choose to send.

Who it's for

Self-employed trades & businesses

Sole traders running their own business — hairdressers, plumbers, consultants, and other trades — who already track income and expenses in a spreadsheet and need MTD ITSA quarterly updates without retyping totals.

UK property landlords

Individuals with UK rental property who maintain period income and expenses locally and must send property business quarterly updates under Making Tax Digital for Income Tax.

Foreign property landlords

Landlords with overseas property income who need foreign property period summaries submitted alongside or instead of UK property — still driven from the same local file.

What “bridging only” means

Under HMRC Making Tax Digital, bridging software takes figures from your existing digital records (here: a local spreadsheet or structured file) and sends them to HMRC. Spreadsheet Tax does not replace your accounts package or force you into a cloud ledger. You stay in control of the file on your machine; we map and bridge the quarterly submission path.

  • Local file upload (CSV / Excel template)
  • Automatic map of income & expense lines for SE and property
  • Preview of HMRC quarterly update payloads before send
  • Digital audit link: spreadsheet field → payload field

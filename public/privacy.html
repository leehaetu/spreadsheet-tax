Privacy notice

This notice explains how Spreadsheet Tax uses personal information when you create an account and use the service.

1. Controller

Lee Hine operates Spreadsheet Tax. Contact via the support channel published for this deployment (see Help).

2. What we process

  • Account email, name, password hash
  • Uploaded spreadsheets and derived drafts for mapping
  • Tax identifiers you choose to save (for example National Insurance number and business IDs) and your HMRC connection details
  • Submission attempts, receipts, and audit events
  • Technical logs (should not include full NINOs or spreadsheet contents)

3. Why

To provide the service you request: authentication, spreadsheet mapping, review, HMRC connection, and evidence of submissions.

4. Sharing

  • HMRC — when you connect and choose to send an update
  • Hosting — infrastructure provider for the deployed app and database
  • Email — only if a delivery provider is configured; otherwise messages may not leave the server

5. Retention

Account and submission records are kept while the account is active and as required for tax evidence. Deletion process will be refined with legal review; contact support to request erasure where applicable.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you may have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. You may complain to the ICO.

7. Contact

Use Help. Do not send National Insurance numbers by unsolicited email when avoidable.

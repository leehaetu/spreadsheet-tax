What this product is

Spreadsheet Tax is bridging software for HMRC Making Tax Digital for Income Tax. It maps period figures from a spreadsheet into quarterly update payloads shaped for HMRC MTD APIs. It is not HMRC-recognised software and is not offered for live recognised filing until recognition is granted. It is not a full double-entry ledger.

Data we process

  • Account data — email, name, password hash, session cookies
  • Period file content — figures you upload for mapping (income, expenses, period dates)
  • Tax identifiers you enter — National Insurance number, HMRC business IDs, tax year (when provided for submit)
  • Submission records — draft and attempt metadata, preview or HMRC responses
  • Practice data — firm name, client display names, workflow status, due dates (when you use the workspace)
  • Technical data — IP address, user agent, device identifiers used for HMRC fraud-prevention headers

Why we process it

  • To provide the bridging service you request
  • To authenticate you and protect accounts
  • To prepare and (when enabled) send HMRC MTD submissions with required fraud-prevention headers
  • To operate practice workspace features you use

HMRC

When live or sandbox HMRC submission is enabled and you connect OAuth and submit, relevant data is sent to HMRC under HMRC’s systems and terms. Default public demo mode uses a preview path that does not call HMRC. See software integrity.

Storage and retention

Production data is stored on the application host volume (SQLite) under the service data directory. You may delete drafts from history when signed in. Session cookies expire. A fuller retention schedule will ship with the production GDPR pack.

## 1. Strategic position (current statement)

**Defensible position (working):**

> The fastest controlled route from an existing spreadsheet to an accountant-reviewed MTD update, without migrating the client into a full bookkeeping platform.

**Central product promise:**

> Keep using your spreadsheet. Review your figures clearly. Send your MTD updates with confidence.

### We will / we will not (first launch)

| We will | We will not initially |
|---------|------------------------|
| Serve spreadsheet-based sole traders and landlords | Build full bookkeeping or general ledger software |
| Support accountants as a distribution channel | Compete feature-for-feature with large accounting suites |
| Make mapping, review and evidence exceptional | Support every tax-return income source at launch |
| Build secure multi-client workflows | Offer unauthenticated live submission |
| Focus on UK MTD ITSA | Expand internationally before UK product-market fit |
| License companies to resell (IP Lee Hine) | Give away IP or unrestricted modification rights |

## 2. Launch beachhead recommendation

### Recommended primary launch market

**Bookkeepers and small accountancy practices managing ~20–200 spreadsheet-based self-employed and landlord clients.**

**Why (hypothesis to validate):**

| Factor | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|
| Contract value | Higher ARPU than single Personal seats |
| Repeat usage | Every quarter, many clients |
| Distribution | One pro → many end users |
| Access | Direct line to individuals via portal/import |
| Pain | Spreadsheet clients do not want full ledger migration |

**Personal subscriptions** remain a product and sales path, but **professional distribution is the preferred commercial entry** unless interviews falsify this.

### Deliberately excluded from first launch

| Exclude | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Complex partnerships / corporations beyond ITSA SE + property | Scope |
| Non-UK tax regimes | Focus |
| Full CIS, VAT suite as primary product | Not bridging MVP |
| Enterprises needing deep PMS integrations | Later |
| Users who refuse any file upload | Architecture requires server mapping (honest privacy) |
